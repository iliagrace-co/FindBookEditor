Common questions.

Who uses this directory?

Nonfiction authors — coaches, consultants, memoir writers, wellness practitioners, first-time authors — who are actively looking for editorial support. Our audience comes to us because they have a manuscript and they need help.

What kinds of editors are listed?

We focus on nonfiction editorial services: developmental editing, line editing, editorial polish, manuscript assessment, ghostwriting, and proofreading. Fiction specialists can apply but will be reviewed individually.

How do I get listed?

Email us at hello@ugepress.com with your name, specialty, services, and website. We review all applications and respond within 48 hours. Listings go live immediately upon payment.

Is there a commission on client bookings?

No. Your listing fee is flat. We don't take a cut of your client work. Ever.

Can I cancel anytime?

Listings are annual (non-refundable after 30 days), but you can update your profile at any time during the year.
